Future-Forward Transparency: Pioneering an Open-Standard Schema at AI Cyber Labs

At AI Cyber Labs, we're not just building an AI Education Platform; we're aspiring to cultivate a world-class, open-source ecosystem that empowers collaboration and innovation in cybersecurity. Our vision includes introducing a standardized schema that revolutionizes how cybersecurity professionals interact with AI and ML. This forward-looking plan entails enabling users to import their own datasets for nuanced analysis on our platform, as well as access specialized, cyber-focused datasets for independent research.

Our proposed schema is designed to ensure uniformity across models, employing binary labels to facilitate clear, swift analysis through F1 type scoring. This approach is meticulously aligned with the urgent needs of cybersecurity defenders, who require the ability to rapidly distinguish between normal and suspicious data.

Furthermore, our commitment to transparency extends to our models' source code. We plan to make all code openly available for scrutiny, allowing users to understand, analyze, and contribute to improvements. This initiative, though in its nascent stages as part of our MVP development, underscores our dedication to an open, collaborative future in AI-enhanced cybersecurity.

By paving the way for an open-standard ecosystem, AI Cyber Labs aims to set a new benchmark in accessible, transparent AI research and application in the cybersecurity domain.
