About Us

Our UC Berkeley Capstone Project squad is where talent meets tenacity, juggling a carnival of roles like strategic planning, software engineering, development, cybersecurity, machine learning, and project management, all with a dash of fun! Diving headfirst into the challenge, we bootstrapped our way through, crafting our app entirely in-house, proving that innovation isn’t just about resources—it’s about creativity, collaboration, and a bit of play. This journey underscores our team’s spirited dynamism, ready to tackle complex tasks with smiles and smarts, turning ambitious ideas into exciting realities.
